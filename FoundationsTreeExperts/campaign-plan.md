## Campaign Structure

At $450/month, run **two campaigns** — a primary lead gen campaign and a retargeting campaign. Don't spread thinner than this.

### Campaign 1: Lead Generation (80% = $360/month, ~$12/day)
- **Objective:** Leads (conversion location: Website)
- **Destination:** foundationstreeexperts.com
- **Audience:** Ann Arbor core, 5-10 mile radius
  - Age: 35-65
  - Homeowners (Detailed Targeting > Demographics > Home > Homeownership > Homeowners)
  - Interests: Home improvement, Landscaping, Gardening, Lawn care
- **Placements:** Advantage+ (let Meta optimize across Feed, Stories, Reels)
- **Ad Format:** Mix of single image and short video
- **CTA Button:** Get Quote → foundationstreeexperts.com

### Campaign 2: Retargeting (20% = $90/month, ~$3/day)
- **Objective:** Traffic (optimize for Landing Page Views)
- **Destination:** foundationstreeexperts.com
- **Audience:** Custom Audiences
  - Website visitors (last 60 days)
  - Facebook/Instagram engagers (last 90 days)
  - Video viewers (watched 25%+ of any video ad)
- **Ad Format:** Single image with testimonial overlay or different angle from Campaign 1
- **Purpose:** Catch the 97% who saw you but didn't act

### Campaign 3: Storm Response (Dormant — $0 until activated)
- Pre-built, paused
- Activate within hours of a severe weather event
- Temporarily increase daily budget to $25-30/day during storm week (pull from Campaign 1)
- Deactivate after 5-7 days

## Budget Math

| Item | Monthly | Daily |
|------|---------|-------|
| Lead Gen | $360 | $12 |
| Retargeting | $90 | $3 |
| **Total** | **$450** | **$15** |

**Expected results at $450/month:**
- CPL estimate: $50-80 (website leads cost more but close better)
- Expected leads (calls + form fills): 6-9/month
- At even a 25% close rate on $2,500 avg job = $3,750-$5,625 revenue
- ROI: 8-13x ad spend

**Why website over Instant Forms:** Higher-intent leads. Someone who clicks through to the site, reads the page, and picks up the phone or fills out the form is genuinely interested — not just tapping "submit" on autopilot. Fewer leads, but far better close rate.

**Critical success factor:** The website must convert. Phone number prominent, quote form easy to find, mobile-optimized. The ad gets them there — the site closes them.

## Creative Rotation

Run 3-4 ads per campaign at a time. Rotate new creative every 2-3 weeks to prevent ad fatigue. At this budget, Meta needs a small ad set to find winners — don't run more than 4 simultaneously.

### Recommended rotation schedule:
- **Weeks 1-3:** Launch with Ad Sets A (see ad-copy.md)
- **Weeks 4-6:** Swap underperformers for Ad Sets B
- **Weeks 7-9:** Introduce seasonal angle
- **Week 10+:** Scale what's working, kill what isn't

## Measurement & Optimization

### Week 1-2: Learning Phase
- Don't touch anything. Meta's algorithm needs ~50 events to exit learning phase
- At $12/day and ~$50 CPL, this takes about 4 weeks — be patient

### Key Metrics to Watch
| Metric | Good | Investigate |
|--------|------|-------------|
| CPL (Cost Per Lead) | Under $60 | Over $90 |
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | Over 1.5% | Under 0.8% |
| Pixel Lead Events | Firing consistently | Gaps or missing events |
| Frequency | Under 3 | Over 5 (audience fatigue) |

### Monthly Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Review CPL by ad — pause anything over 2x your average CPL
- [ ] Check frequency — if over 4, rotate creative
- [ ] Review lead quality with David — are website leads converting to jobs?
- [ ] Verify pixel lead events are firing in Events Manager
- [ ] Check placement breakdown — where are leads coming from?
