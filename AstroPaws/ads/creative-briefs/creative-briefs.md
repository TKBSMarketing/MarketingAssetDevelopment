# Astro Paws -- Ad Creative Briefs

Direction for ad image/video creation. Each brief describes what to build, what assets to use, what sizes to deliver, and what text to overlay. The client (Maka Games) should create these using the existing card art as the primary visual material.

---

## Design System (Use Across All Creatives)

**Color palette:**
- Primary background: `#1a1d2e` (dark navy -- matches existing card art backgrounds)
- Kickstarter green: `#05ce78` (for CTA buttons and badges)
- Micro May gold: `#f5c518` (for event badges and ribbons)
- Character accent colors: Blue `#4a7fb5`, Red `#d45a3a`, Green `#3a8a4a`, Yellow `#e8a030`, Purple `#8a5ab0`

**Typography:**
- Use a bold, rounded sans-serif font (Nunito Bold, Baloo 2, or similar) -- matches the playful space theme
- Headlines: Bold, large, white or light text with subtle glow/shadow for readability on dark backgrounds
- Body text: Regular weight, white, clean
- Avoid thin or serif fonts -- they don't match the game's personality

**Visual principles:**
- Dark space backgrounds with subtle stars (consistent with card art)
- Cards should have a soft glow/shadow effect to pop off the background
- Keep compositions clean -- the card art is strong enough to carry the visual, don't over-design
- Every ad should include the Astro Paws logo (from the card back design)
- Every ad should include a Kickstarter reference (logo, "Coming to Kickstarter," or green CTA bar)
- Mobile-first: all text must be readable at phone screen size

**Delivery sizes:**
- Facebook landscape: 1200 x 628 px
- Facebook/Instagram square: 1080 x 1080 px
- Instagram story: 1080 x 1920 px (or 1080 x 1350 for feed)

## Creative 1: "Hero Character Lineup"

**Priority: HIGH -- This is the primary ad image. Build this first.**

**Concept:** All 5 "Play Here" character cards arranged in a fan or overlapping lineup, slightly tilted. The characters are the stars -- let them sell the game.

**Assets to use:**
- `1 Play Here - Blue.jpg`
- `1 Play Here - Red.jpg`
- `1 Play Here - Green.jpg`
- `1 Play Here - Yellow.jpg`
- `1 Play Here - Purple.jpg`

**Layout direction:**
- Fan the 5 cards across the center of the frame, overlapping slightly, each tilted 5-10 degrees from the next
- Red panda (Red) should be front/center -- it's the most visually distinctive character
- Cards should be large enough that the character illustrations are clearly visible
- Astro Paws logo above the card fan
- Tagline below: **"The Cutest Card Game in Space"**
- Small Kickstarter badge or "Coming to Kickstarter" text in bottom corner
- Background: dark navy with subtle star particles

**Text overlay:**
- Top: Astro Paws logo
- Center: Card fan (no text needed -- art speaks)
- Bottom: "The Cutest Card Game in Space"
- Corner: "Coming to Kickstarter | #MicroMay"

**Deliver in:** All 3 sizes (landscape, square, story)

**Pair with:** Ad Copy Variation 1 (Introduction) or Variation 2 (Cute Art Lead)

## Creative 4: "Card Fan + Feature Bullets"

**Priority: HIGH -- Best conversion format for Facebook feed ads.**

**Concept:** Split composition. Left side shows cards fanned out (the visual hook). Right side lists key features in clean, scannable bullet points. This is the "direct response" format that drives clicks.

**Assets to use:**
- `1 Play Here - Blue.jpg`
- `1 Play Here - Red.jpg`
- `1 Play Here - Yellow.jpg`

**Layout direction (landscape 1200x628):**
- LEFT 55%: Three cards fanned against dark space background, tilted slightly, with glow shadows
- RIGHT 45%: Slightly lighter dark panel (`#232840` or similar) with:
  - Astro Paws logo at top
  - Feature bullets with small icons:
    - "2-5 Players"
    - "15-Minute Games"
    - "Easy to Learn"
    - "Fits in Your Pocket"
    - "Ages 8+"
  - Green CTA button: "Get Notified on Kickstarter"
- Thin divider line or subtle gradient between left and right panels

**Important:** Each feature bullet should be large enough to read on a mobile phone screen. Don't cram too many -- 4-5 bullets maximum.

**Deliver in:** Facebook landscape (1200x628) only. This format doesn't work well in square or story.

**Pair with:** Ad Copy Variation 1 (Introduction) or Variation 5 (Quick Play)

## Creative 5: "Space Taxi" -- Scroll Stopper

**Priority: MEDIUM -- Unique creative for standing out in feed.**

**Concept:** Uses the "Move Elsewhere - Purple" card which shows the purple cat hailing a space taxi. This is the most unusual and charming card in the deck -- it will stop people from scrolling because it's unexpected and delightful.

**Assets to use:**
- `4 Move Elsewhere - Purple.jpg` (hero -- this card must be large and prominent)
- `Blue Back.jpg` (small, for Astro Paws logo reference)

**Layout direction:**
- Card image fills most of the frame (80%+), with rounded corners and a soft glow
- Semi-transparent dark overlay bar at top with text: **"Need a Ride?"** or **"Space Taxi, Anyone?"**
- Bottom bar: **"Astro Paws -- Coming to Kickstarter"** in Kickstarter green
- Keep it simple -- the card image does all the work. Don't clutter.

**The psychology:** This ad works because it makes people curious. "What is this game with a cat hailing a space taxi?" It's designed for engagement and clicks, not hard selling.

**Deliver in:** Square (1080x1080) and story (1080x1920)

**Pair with:** Ad Copy Variation 2 (Cute Art Lead) -- the playful tone matches

## Bonus: Video Direction (If Budget Allows)

Short-form video (15-30 seconds) outperforms on Instagram Reels and can be repurposed for TikTok.

**Simple video concept -- "Card Reveal":**
1. Dark space background with stars (0-3 sec)
2. Cards flip in one by one, each revealing a different character (3-12 sec)
3. Cards fan out to show all 5 characters together (12-15 sec)
4. Text animates in: "Astro Paws -- The Cutest Card Game in Space" (15-20 sec)
5. Kickstarter CTA + #MicroMay badge (20-25 sec)
6. End card with "Coming to Kickstarter" + link (25-30 sec)

**Can be made with:** After Effects, Canva animation, or even PowerPoint animation exported as video. No live footage needed -- the card art is strong enough to carry a motion graphics approach.

**Music:** Upbeat, whimsical, space-themed. Think chiptune or playful synth. Keep it light and fun.

## Testing Strategy

When running these ads on Meta Ads Manager:

1. **Start with Creative 1 + Variation 1 copy** -- this is your baseline performer
2. **A/B test Creative 2** with different hero characters to find the top performer
3. **Run Creative 3 (Micro May) concurrently** -- it has a time window, so start immediately
4. **Add Creative 4 (Feature Bullets) as a second ad set** -- different format may reach different people
5. **Use Creatives 5 and 6 as "creative fatigue" replacements** -- swap them in after 7-10 days when the primary ads start losing performance
6. **Carousel (Creative 7) works best for retargeting** -- people who visited the page but didn't follow/notify

**Budget guidance:** Start with $10-20/day split across 2-3 ad sets. Scale what works. Kill what doesn't after 3 days and 1,000+ impressions with no clicks.

**Targeting layers:**
- Layer 1: Interest in Kickstarter + Interest in board games/card games
- Layer 2: Interest in Kickstarter + Interest in cats/animals/cute things
- Layer 3: Lookalike audience from Kickstarter page followers (once you have enough followers)
