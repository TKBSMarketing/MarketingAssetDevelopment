## Core Marketing Copy (from maka.games/ink)

### Tagline
> "A Card Game of being Sneakier than the other Clever Octopuses"

### Hero Description
> "Bluff, bait, and outwit your rivals as you hide from predators, collect food tiles, and punch fish in the face with your eight-armed arsenal!"

### Central Hook
> "Are you HUNTing for food points... or sending SHARKS to eat your rivals?"

### Gameplay Descriptions
- Play single cards face down near Food piles
- "You got that last FISH...or did you want them to think that?"
- HUNT for tastiest food to form powerful sets
- Lay traps for unsuspecting rivals
- Cause chaos by moving target cards to new locations
- Trigger special abilities cleverly

### Designer Endorsement
> "The game tells a story, and interesting things happen!" -- Jason Tremblay, Board Game Designer

## Game Mechanics
- **Programming** -- Players play cards face down, predicting what opponents will do
- **Bluffing** -- Core mechanic; hidden information and misdirection
- **Set Collection** -- Hunt for food to form powerful scoring sets
- **Take-That** -- Send sharks after rivals, punch fish, lay traps
